## 2. Health & Fitness Data Privacy

- All workout data (session times, durations, calorie estimates, gym locations) is **stored locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework.

- You may revoke HealthKit permissions at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

## 3. Location Data Usage

- GymClock uses **geofencing** (CoreLocation region monitoring) to detect when you arrive at or leave a saved gym location.
- Location data is processed **entirely on-device** by iOS. GymClock does not track, log, or transmit your GPS coordinates.
- Geofencing requires "Always" location authorization. You may downgrade or revoke this at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
- Saved gym coordinates are stored locally and are never shared with third parties.
